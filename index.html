Our Services

At Euforg, we are committed to delivering innovative, customized solutions that enhance everyday life and support sustainable living. Our core areas of expertise include:

Product Development

We specialize in end-to-end design, prototyping, and development of cutting-edge consumer electronics tailored to your unique needs. Our product portfolio includes:

  • Smart Home Devices : Automation and control systems that bring convenience and energy savings to modern households.
  • Agriculture Support Products : Smart irrigation, soil monitoring, and farming aids to boost agricultural efficiency.
  • Life-Saving Devices : Essential safety and emergency tools designed for timely response and protection.
  • Energy-Efficient Products : Innovative electronics focused on reducing energy consumption without compromising performance.

Energy Management Consulting

Our sustainable energy solutions are designed to help you optimize power usage, reduce waste, and transition to greener technologies. Services include:

  • Solar Panel Installation : Customized solar solutions for homes, businesses, and institutions.
  • Energy-Efficient Kitchens : Smart kitchen appliances and layout designs that reduce electricity and gas usage.
  • Water Management Support : Systems to monitor, control, and conserve water in domestic and commercial settings.

Technical Consultancy

We offer expert technical support and guidance to bring your concepts to life and streamline your projects, including:

  • PCB Design & Prototyping : Custom printed circuit board layouts and fabrication assistance.
  • Embedded Systems Development : Firmware design and integration for microcontrollers and IoT devices.
  • Electronics Troubleshooting & Support : Diagnostics, repair advice, and improvements for electronic systems.
  • Training & Mentoring : Workshops and one-on-one sessions for students, startups, and enterprises exploring new technologies.
